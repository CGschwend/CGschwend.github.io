I am a PhD Candidate at ETH Zurich in the Group for Sustainability and Technology. My research focuses on the decarbonization of energy and transport systems across sectors. I currently investigate the integration of low-carbon technologies, such as electric vehicles and heat pumps, and the interaction between transport and electricity systems, including infrastructure, vehicle-to-grid, urban systems, energy storage, demand management, and mobility behavior.

I am currently a visiting PhD student at the Institute for Data, Systems, and Society (IDSS) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), working with Prof. Jessika Trancik.

If you are interested in discussing my research or working with me, feel free to reach out to me: cgschwendtner@ethz.ch
